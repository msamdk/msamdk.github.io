Hi I'm Samitha Thilakarathna

I'm a Masters student specializing in Fisheries, biodiversity, and resource management at Technical University of Denmark (DTU Aqua), focused on Computer vision and AI applications. I am also a Graphic Designer and a 2D animator who contributes to different projects to convey scientific information in a creative and engaging way. Explore my research interests and career on the following pages.

Research Interests

My research primarily focuses on computer vision and deep learning applications. Currently, I am applying these advanced techniques to sustainable fisheries and aquaculture, aiming to address critical challenges in fish catch monitoring. With the rapid evolution of AI, it is imperative to develop innovative solutions that enable fully documented and transparent fisheries operations, ultimately contributing to more regulated and environmentally responsible practices.

Passion

I am deeply passionate about exploring complex concepts in computer vision and their practical applications in real-world systems, particularly through edge computing. My focus lies in developing innovative solutions that leverage lightweight deep learning models, ensuring high efficiency with minimal computational demand. By integrating advanced AI techniques with edge devices, I aim to contribute to the sustainable utilization of energy while enabling real-time processing and decision-making in resource-constrained environments. This approach is particularly crucial in domains such as fisheries, aquaculture, and environmental monitoring, where AI-driven insights can enhance efficiency, automation, and sustainability. Through my work, I strive to push the boundaries of deep learning applications, making cutting-edge AI more accessible, efficient, and impactful across various industries.

Goals

My goal is to advance the field of computer vision by developing intelligent, efficient, and scalable AI solutions that seamlessly integrate with real-world systems.
